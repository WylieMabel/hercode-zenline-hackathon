## What To Compare

- New arrivals.
- Bestseller or ranking hints.
- Repeated product materials, weights, and feature claims.
- Product bundles or kits.
- Rental, repair, resale, or membership services.
- Price bands and discounts.
- Availability gaps for Switzerland or DACH.
