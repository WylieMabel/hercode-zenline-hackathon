# Source Ideas

Use a mix of sources. The goal is not to scrape everything; it is to show that your system can combine signals without losing provenance.

## Search And Trend Sources

- Google Trends or another search-trend provider.
- Rising queries in English and local languages: German, French, Italian.
- Search terms from US, Japan, Korea, Nordics, UK, DACH, and Switzerland.

## Web Research

- Outdoor trade publications.
- Outdoor brand launch pages.
- Retailer category pages.
- Event pages for trail running, hiking, camping, and alpine sports.
- Forum and community discussions.

## Social And Community Signals

- TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts, Reddit, Discord, and creator newsletters.
- Hashtags around hiking, trail running, gorpcore, repair, rental, ultralight, and microadventures.
- Creator posts that show product usage, not only product promotion.

## Marketplace And Retail Signals

- Bestseller pages.
- New-arrivals pages.
- Product review counts and rating velocity.
- Discount patterns and out-of-stock indicators.
- Repeated product formats across multiple retailers.

## Local Fit Checks

- Swiss/DACH climate and seasonality.
- Local outdoor behavior and transport patterns.
- Availability of brands or substitutes.
- Price plausibility in CHF.
- Whether the idea fits store operations, supplier access, or merchandising.
